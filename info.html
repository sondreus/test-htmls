Personal Daily Briefing

This little app is meant as a proof of concept. It attempts to summarize recent data generated by The Economist and present it in a way that is only possible via generative AI.

The ambition is to suggest a product that:

  • can incorporate any information produced by editorial, not just articles
  • can be personalized, for instance to only present information that has emerged since the viewer last opened the app, but potentially based on geography, demography, or interests
  • can leverage future advances in AI by improving vastly as LLMs do
  • can be an entirely new (and perhaps separate) product
  • can rely on information that is vetted and if needed (and initially) only display journalist-checked nuggets of information

It is put together by first having an LLM summarize all recent Economist coverage (for now, only articles), and then pick out major themes within it. It next selects bullet points summarizing this content efficiently, and groups it under the major themes, linking to the underlying content. This system is already operational, but for now, updates manually.
